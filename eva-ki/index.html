Continual Learning

Training Machine Learning models from sequentially-arriving data.

Continual learning means the training of models with sequentially-received small amounts of data from different sources. In contrast to the usual workflow of training and evaluating Machine Learning methods, continuous learning algorithms can process successively incoming data, which may be available only for a short period of time.
