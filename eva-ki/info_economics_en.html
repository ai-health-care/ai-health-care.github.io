Health economics and Ethics


Before systems for automated diagnostic support can be deployed across the board, the data basis for developing such systems must be standardized and the precise effects on patient care, physician workflows and direct and indirect costs in the healthcare system must be examined. The goal of the EVA-KI project is to establish a platform that meets these requirements.

The results of the project are to be used sustainably to further strengthen the quality and efficiency of German health care. An elementary component of the project is therefore the development and verification of an economic business model for the widespread use of the platform.
