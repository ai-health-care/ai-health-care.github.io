Structured Reporting


Up to now, medical findings have mainly been recorded in the form of free text. As a result, the findings often differ considerably in style and scope, depending on the personal preference of the treating physician. This lack of standardization and structuring in data acquisition means that the data collected cannot be evaluated directly.

Smart Reporting has developed an innovative system that queries relevant medical decisions in a targeted manner, thereby achieving a far-reaching standardization. Thanks to the system's intelligent data acquisition, the meaning of each individual statement of findings is clear and facilitates the exchange and interpretation of medical findings. The data collected by the system is directly available for analysis, and artificial intelligence methods use this data to gain new insights to improve diagnosis.
