hessian.AI health.care

First Hessian conference on AI-ready health care

hessian.AI health.care is the inaugural Hessian virtual conference on AI for health care. It will take place on 23rd of November 2020 and is organized by the Medical Computing group at GRIS, TU Darmstadt. To keep all participants safe during the time of the COVID-19 gist of a pandemic, this conference will take place in an online meeting via Zoom.
